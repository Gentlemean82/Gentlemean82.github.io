1. INTRODUCTION

In 2000 there was not even one expert organization in this particular field of Land Surveying except Survey of Pakistan. It was vital to establish an expert standard organization that works for better & fast development based on accurate surveys. Standard Land Surveyors was established in 2000 and named “Standard Surveyors” which was an incomplete name for “Standard Land Surveyors” by two partners as Directors named Mr. Chauhdary Nazar-ul-Haq & Mr. Raja Muhammad Ahmad. Chaudhary Nazar-ul-Haq has a Diploma in Civil Engineering from Rasul College (1980-1983) and 33 Years of working experience in Survey of Pakistan (1985-2000).

Raja Muhammad Ahmad has a Certificate in Surveying from the Punjab Board of Technical Education, Lahore (1964-1965) and 35 Years of working experience in Capital Development Authority Islamabad and retired from the post of Assistant Director in 2000. Standard Land Surveyors registered from Company Registrar Islamabad in 2000 & applied as Engineering Firm from Pakistan Engineering Council recently. Company N.T.N is 2746719-8. Standard Land Surveyors A/C # 017701000680, Bank Alfalah Limited, Khana Pul, Rawalpindi.
